A notes app that doesn't require a PhD.

Productivity Page is a powerful and user-friendly planner that syncs across all your IOS devices seamlessly. Now including Jake, your productivity assistant! Jake can help streamline your tasks, manage your calendar, and keep your projects on track with ease!

Features

iCloud Support

Your notes are securely stored in the cloud and updated in real time. Enjoy effortless note management and backup without ever worrying about losing your data.

Cross Platform

Your notes automatically sync on your iPhone, iPad, and Mac! This seamless integration means your ideas are always at your fingertips, no matter which device you're using.

Jake Chat

Introducing Jake, an AI assistant who can help you stay productive! Jake not only organizes your tasks but also offers personalized suggestions to boost your efficiency.
